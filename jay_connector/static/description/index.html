Jay - AI Voice CRM Assistant

Manage your Odoo CRM with your voice. No more manual data entry.

What is Jay?

Jay is an AI-powered voice assistant built for sales teams. Record a voice note describing your meeting, and Jay automatically creates or updates contacts, companies, opportunities, activities, and calendar events in your Odoo CRM.

Key Features

  • Voice-to-CRM - Speak naturally, Jay understands and syncs to Odoo
  • Contacts & Companies - Create, update, and search with anti-duplicate detection
  • Opportunities - Create deals, move stages, mark as won/lost
  • Activities - Schedule tasks with natural language dates
  • Calendar Events - Create and manage meetings
  • Notes - Add notes to contacts, companies, and opportunities
  • Chrome Extension - Quick access from your browser
  • WhatsApp Integration - Send voice notes via WhatsApp
  • Multilingual - French, English, and Dutch supported

How it works

  1. Install this module and enter your Jay API credentials in Settings
  2. Record a voice note in the Jay app or via WhatsApp
  3. Jay analyzes your message and identifies CRM actions
  4. Contacts, deals, activities, and events are synced to Odoo automatically

Anti-duplicate Intelligence

Jay uses smart matching algorithms to detect existing contacts and companies before creating new records. This keeps your Odoo database clean and avoids duplicates, even when names are slightly different.
